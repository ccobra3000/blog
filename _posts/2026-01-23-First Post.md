The first project that I've actually had in use for a couple years, but plan on upgrading is my bird house camera. I'm currently calling this project Nestflix, and at this point it is running on a RPi 4B with an Arducam Hawkeye.
The current code will wait until I remote in to either run a live stream, record a short video or just take a screenshot. Since it's usually a remote spot, I had been running it on a simple battery pack for cell phones and just plugging it in to check 
on nesting status, bird identification and brood health. Upcoming posts will include the upgrade from just a mobile battery pack to using solar with timed recordings and photos.
